Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a car? +
You'll need a valid driver's license, a major credit card in your name, and proof of insurance. International renters may need an International Driving Permit.
What is your cancellation policy? +
You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee.
Do you offer roadside assistance? +
Yes! We provide 24/7 roadside assistance for all our rentals. Just call our emergency hotline at +250 794 098 499.
Can I modify my booking after confirmation? +
Absolutely! You can modify your booking dates, car type, or pickup location through your profile page or by contacting our support team.
